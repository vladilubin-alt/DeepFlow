## Listing 1: ADHD Focus (Primary)

**Title (30 chars):** DeepFlow — Focus Timer
**Short Desc (80 chars):** ADHD-friendly focus writing app with forgiving guillotine & grace tokens.

**Long Description:**

Focus writing app designed for ADHD minds that need structure without the shame spiral. DeepFlow is the distraction-free writing timer that forgives your humanity.

Stop fighting your brain. The Forgiving Guillotine gives you a 10-second fuse — if you pause, the timer doesn't punish you. Grace Tokens protect your streak when life gets in the way. Every session builds momentum, not guilt.

**Features that actually work with ADHD:**
- Focus timer with visual progress — see your flow state grow
- Grace Tokens: 3 per day to save your streak without penalty
- Binaural audio focus layers (Alpha/Theta) — put on headphones and disappear
- Focus Score: real-time feedback on your writing velocity
- Streak calendar: watch your consistency grow, one session at a time
- Time blindness timer — visual cues keep you oriented without clock anxiety
- Midnight Luxe dark mode — easy on the eyes during late-night sprints

Built by someone who knows what it's like to stare at a blank page for 45 minutes. No judgement. Just flow.

## Listing 4: Time Blindness (Niche)

**Title (30 chars):** DeepFlow — Time Blindness Timer
**Short Desc (80 chars):** Time blindness timer for ADHD — visual flow state tracking without clock anxiety.

**Long Description:**

Time blindness is real. DeepFlow replaces the anxiety of watching a clock with a visual flow-state indicator that keeps you oriented without breaking your focus.

No countdown. No numbers. Just a growing orb that shows your progress. When you're in flow, the timer fades into the background. When you drift, the guillotine gently reminds you.

**Built for neurodivergent minds:**
- Visual timer: see time as a growing shape, not shrinking numbers
- Forgiving Guillotine: 10-second pause window before session ends
- Grace Tokens: protect your streak without penalty
- Binaural audio: alpha/beta waves for deep focus
- Focus Score: actionable feedback on your writing velocity

Not a Pomodoro clone. Not a habit tracker. A writing instrument for people whose brains work differently.
